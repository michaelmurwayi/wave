Our Projects

Hopestar Academy

In Masai Mara, around 75 percent of students do not have a desk or a chair.We are commited to provide that missing piece of classroom equipment that can make all the difference for young learners.

Information center

Technology is the window of opportunity. In partnership with Orphaned Starfish Foundation, we fully equipped an Information Center with the IT equipment needed for children to have virtual classes and learn advanced computer skills.

Greenhouse Project

The Children at the home love learning about the process of agriculture, and helping plant their harvest.Not only is this project a wonderful learning experience, it allows us to be sustainable and have a harvest that directly feeds our home.
