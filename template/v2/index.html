WAVE Children’s Foundation is an international nonprofit organization, working in collaboration with underserved communities in Africa to bring about sustainable solutions for everyday challenges. We work in food and education programs, supporting children rescue centers and orphanages with a variety of integrated projects focusing on basic needs. All our sustainable community development projects are Africa -led; however, we are also committed to empowering international volunteers to see themselves as global citizens and agents of change.

At WAVE, we are more than an organization – we are a community united by love and a shared vision for a brighter tomorrow.

About Us

At WAVE, we are dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of children and families in need. Our foundation was founded with the belief that every child deserves a happy and healthy childhood, regardless of their circumstances. We are committed to creating a better future for young minds by providing essential support, education, and opportunities.

Our mission is to empower children and families by providing them with the tools, resources, and support they need to thrive. We work tirelessly to break down barriers that hinder children's development, ensuring that they have access to quality education, proper healthcare, and a safe environment. Through our various programs and initiatives, we strive to create a world where every child's potential can be fully realized.

Through our food programs support, we ensure that vulnetable kids have access to balanced diet that keeps them fit to grab the opportunities that arise in their journey of growth.Through your support and donation, we shall be able to reach out to more kids and secure the future of a whole generation.
