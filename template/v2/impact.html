Our Impact

Information center at Children's Garden Home

Technology is the window of opportunity. In partnership with Orphaned Starfish Foundation, we fully equipped an Information Center with the IT equipment needed for 20 children to have virtual classes and learn advanced computer skills. Providing the children with the tools and resources needed to advance in today’s modern world will open doors for them filled with career opportunities and educational experiences they otherwise would not have. Since the commencement of this program in mid 2020, we have enhanced the classes for the students, the programs with virtual lessons from all over the world. The kids are now actively involved in coding and lego activities.

Greenhouse at Children's Garden Home

The center supports youth through love, spiritual care, rehabilitation and access to education in order to nourish each child’s personal development. The center serves between 250-500 children. The community not only offers schooling but also empowerment and life-skills programs that support the youth beyond life at the center. Our Greenhouse Project requires ongoing maintenance to upkeep our crops. The Children at the home love learning about the process of agriculture, and helping plant their harvest. Vegetables range depending on the season, with a current focus on tomatoes. Not only is this project a wonderful learning experience, it allows us to be sustainable and have a harvest that directly feeds our home.
