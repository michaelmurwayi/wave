Want to join us in supporting a good cause?

We are working with Children rescue centers and orphanages to offer support to kids living there. Through different programs that we've setup the kids learn how to become responsible citizens. Our programs ensure that the rescue centers become sustainable and can fully support themselves Set up a one-time gift or recurring payments so that you can give on a regular basis to WAVE Children's Foundation! Making a general donation is greatly appreciated and will go to help set up new programs and support what we're currently doing. Thank you for your kind gesture and we would like to encourage you to help us by sharing our campaign! Join us today and make this kids smile.
