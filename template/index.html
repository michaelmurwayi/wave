About

Us

WAVE Children’s Foundation is an international nonprofit organization, working in collaboration with underserved communities in Africa to bring about sustainable solutions for everyday challenges. We work in food and education programs, supporting children rescue centers and orphanages with a variety of integrated projects focusing on basic needs. All our sustainable community development projects are Africa -led; however, we are also committed to empowering international volunteers to see themselves as global citizens and agents of change. WAVE Children’s Foundation sees education as the key to breaking the cycle of poverty..

Our Programs

WAVE Children’s Foundation is a diverse team of passionate leaders, committed to working cooperatively for sustainable programs. Sustainability is more than just completed projects, it is providing hope and empowerment for our impact centers in Kenya. WAVE Children’s Foundation has been partnering with local leaders in Nairobi, Kenya since 2019 providing integrated and sustainable community development projects.

Hopestar Academy is need of desks

In Masai Mara, around 75 percent of students do not have a desk or a chair. We are commited to provide that missing piece of classroom equipment that can make all the difference for young learners.
