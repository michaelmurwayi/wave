We Provide Vulnerable Children in Kenya With Basic Needs.

Wave Children's Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) EIN 85-2738630 charitable organization that supports children rescue centers and orphanages across the world with our first project in Kenya

Offering support and essential goods to those who need it the most.

Since 2019, we have been striving to better the lives of children living in orphanages and rescue centers by offering self substainable programs that aid them in bettering their lives and the communities they come from.

Support Our Cause.

Make a donation and support our Project. We ensure vulnerable kids have access to balance diet to provide the needed energy to grab opportunities in their journey and be a difference in the society.
