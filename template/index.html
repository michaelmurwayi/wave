About

Us

WAVE Children’s Foundation is an international nonprofit organization, working in collaboration with underserved communities in Africa to bring about sustainable solutions for everyday challenges. We work in food and education programs, supporting children rescue centers and orphanages with a variety of integrated projects focusing on basic needs. All our sustainable community development projects are Africa -led; however, we are also committed to empowering international volunteers to see themselves as global citizens and agents of change. WAVE Children’s Foundation sees education as the key to breaking the cycle of poverty..
